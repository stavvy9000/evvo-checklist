Legal, compliance and trust external service
Non-negotiable before any paying customer
0/7▾
Incorporate as a UK limited company at Companies House
£12 online, takes 24 hours. Do this before you take any money.
Register UK trademark for Evvo name and logo
£170 via the IPO. Apply as soon as you are certain of the name.
Get Privacy Policy and Terms of Service written
Use a solicitor or a service like Termly. Budget £300–500. Must be live before any real user touches the product.
GDPR compliance — cookie consent, data processing agreements, right to deletion
You are handling business data for UK and EU customers. Non-compliance carries serious fines. Get advice early.
Register with the ICO as a data controller
Mandatory in the UK if you process personal data. Costs £40–60 per year. Takes about 20 minutes.
Get a freelance developer to audit Supabase Row Level Security
Budget 2–3 hours of developer time. One customer seeing another's data is a catastrophic trust failure. Worth every penny before launch.
Set up a status page showing platform uptime
Use a free tool like Instatus or BetterUptime. Enterprise buyers will ask for this.
